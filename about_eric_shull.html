Background/History

A lot of people ask me: how did you start making instruments? I began playing guitar when I was 20, and not long after that I became fascinated with the different sounds of instruments, and what gave them their unique sound. After about 7 years playing guitar, I decided I wanted to build a guitar. Lucky for me I enjoy research as much as I enjoy building.

So I began to research how to construct a guitar and I quickly learned that gluing together a bunch of pieces of wood to look like a guitar was relatively easy. The difficulty was in the details of tone, finish quaility, playability and expression of personal vision.
